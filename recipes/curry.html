Curry

Description

Red curry is a dish that pairs heat with balaced spices and creaminess from coconut milk.

Ingredients

  • Coconut milk
  • Thai Chilies
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Apple
  • Protien of personal preference

Steps

    Saute onions, and additional vegetables to release water content
    Add required spices
    Saute until fragrant
    Add protien of choice and cook through
    Add coconut milk and finishing spices
    Bring entire contents to boil and reduce until desired level and consistancy
