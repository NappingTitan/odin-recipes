Chili

Description

A hearty meat based dish with added peppers and onions. For a warming dish on a cold night.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • Onions
  • green peppers
  • beef broth
  • black beans
  • tomates

Steps

    brown beef in bottom of large pot
    add in onions and peppers
    add tomatoes
    add beef broth and water
    add beans and reduce down to desired consistancy
    Serve and enjoy
